Russia is still killing Ukrainians. Danieli is still doing business with Russia.

The Italian international industrial company, Danieli, is one of the three largest suppliers of equipment for the metalworking industry in the world.

The Danieli firm produces machinery for metallurgical plants, including blast furnaces, rolling mills for continuous casting of hot and cold metal products, cutting tools, and devices for automating the metallurgical process at every level.

30 percent of the company's global revenue is generated in Russia. There are service centers for Danieli in Magnitogorsk, Chelyabinsk, Vyksa, Dzerzhynsk, and Novokuznetsk in addition to the company's head office in Moscow. The company's presence is not geographically random. Large Russian industrial plants, Danielш's clients, are situated in these cities. In Russia, a specialized manufacturing called "Danieli Volga" is also in operation.

Formally, Danieli's clients do not belong to the military-industrial complex. However, they are the main suppliers of raw materials (for example, special types of steel) to Russian military industrial complex enterprises that manufacture weapons.

Yes, it is difficult to categorically label Danieli's machines as those having a military purpose. However, Russia uses Danieli’s equipment to make tank armor, nuclear reactor hulls on nuclear submarines, elements of salvo-fire systems, and more.

The most important thing is that, even after Russia started a bloody war in Europe, Danieli did not refuse to cooperate with the aggressor. Of course, if someone sells lemons, then what difference does it make to him or her, who will drink lemonade squeezed from them. However, does this mean that it is worth selling lemons to a concentration camp?

What do Danielі's Russian clients do?

Danieli works with the major players in the Russian metallurgical industry. Some of them are owned by Vladimir Putin-connected Russian oligarchs who have been sanctioned. The great majority collaborates with businesses in the Russian defense sector and takes part in the direct production of weapons for the Russian military.

JSC "Corporation Krasny Oktyabr"

The Krasny Oktyabr metallurgical plant is one of the largest Russian producers of special grades of steel used for military purposes. The owner of the plant, Dmitri Pumpiansky, has been under EU and US sanctions since March 9, 2022.

Krasny Oktyabr supplies its own products to the only tank manufacturer in Russia - Uralvagonzavod. The latter uses it for the armor of the T-14 "Armata" tank. The United Aircraft Corporation, which makes SU-57 fighters, also buys steel from Krasny Oktyabr. The steel produced by Krasny Oktyabr is used in the production of nuclear reactor hulls that power Russian nuclear submarines.

In April 2022, the head of JSC "Corporation Krasny Oktyabr" Konstantin Volkov stated that Danieli continued cooperation with the plant. "We had concerns about continuing cooperation with Danieli's company. However, our partners have decided to continue working together and we have already received an offer from them for the purchase of spare parts and are waiting for a contract to launch the PN-370 machine," said Volkov.

Indeed, even two months after the war started by Russia, Danieli continues to supply equipment to the aggressor country. This equipment, in particular, produces special grades of steel and metallurgical products, which are then used for the production of fighter jets, nuclear submarines and tanks by the enterprises of the Russian military industry.

Severstal

The “Severstal” plant supplies more than ten grades of steel to enterprises of the Russian defense industry. In order to be able to produce it, "Severstal" buys machine tools from Danieli and orders the modernization of steel vacuuming technologies at all stages - from project development to supply, installation and maintenance of equipment. Do you know what the future fate of the steel produced under Danieli's technological supervision is? It is used in the construction of new submarines and ships for the Russian Navy, armored personnel carriers and other light armored vehicles, salvo-fire systems.

Special hull steel from "Severstal" is used for the construction of submarines of project 955A ("BoreyA") and project 885 ("Yasen"). Also, “Severstal” is a supplier of special steel for the construction of a series of small missile ships of project 21631: "Grad Sviyazhsk", "Uglich", "Zelenyi dol", "Serpukhov", "Vyshniy Volochok" and "Velikiy Ustyug".

Magnitogorsk Iron and Steel Works (abbreviated as MMK)

The Magnitogorsk Iron and Steel Works is seen in cooperation with KAMAZ PJSC, which belongs to Rostec State Corporation. The enterprise produces military equipment, in particular KamAZ-63968 or "Typhoon-K". The owner of MMK Viktor Rashnikov is under EU sanctions.

Shortly before the Russian invasion, on January 18, 2022, Danieli entered into an agreement with MMK to build four new heating furnaces. The first of them should be commissioned in 2023, and the last - in 2026.

Ural Mining and Metallurgical Company (UMMC)

UMMC Holding, which is a Danieli's partner, owns the Czech company Aircraft Industries. Four transport-passenger aircraft L-410UVPE20 manufactured by Aircraft Industries were handed over to the Aviation Regiment of the Central Military District of Russia.

Kamensk-Uralsky Metallurgical Works J.S.Co. (KUMZ)

KUMZ supplies aluminum parts for both civil and military Russian aircraft, including the Superjet, IL-96, Tu-204/214, Su and MiG class aircraft.

Zlatoust Metallurgical Plant (ZMZ)

Danieli started working with the Zlatoust Metallurgical Plant in 2020. From April 2022, ZMZ products are being supplied to Nizhny Novgorod 70th Anniversary Of Victory Plant (JSC Concern VKO "Almaz-Antey") as part of the import substitution program, which manufactures equipment for the extraction of hydrocarbons to the order of PJSC "Gazprom", as well as to leading oil and gas production companies and oil service enterprises, such as Sibur PJSC, Lukoil PJSC, Transneft PJSC, etc.

No retreat from the Russian market, cancellation of investments or new projects, restriction of business operations, or other reduction in presence in Russia have been announced by Danieli. Additionally, the business keeps on hiring people in Russia.

On June 15, 2022, a meeting of representatives of the Italian company Danieli with representatives of the St. Petersburg business community took place at the Center for the Development and Support of Entrepreneurship. They discussed a number of issues related to production at Russian sites, including new supply chains.

It's interesting to note that during the 2022 economic forum in St. Petersburg, the program of the Chechen delegation on behalf of Ramzan Kadyrov planned to sign a number of important agreements and hold meetings with the President of the Danieli company in Russia.
